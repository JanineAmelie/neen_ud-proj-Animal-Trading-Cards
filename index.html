Jungle Carpet Python

Pythonidae W: 1.5m - 2.6m LOC: Australia

Although fierce looking, this beautiful python is non-venemous to humans.

  • Scientific Name: Morelia spilota cheynei
  • Diet: Medium sized rodents/birds
  • Life Span: 20 years
  • Clutch Size: Up to 18 eggs

Carpet pythons are medium-sized pythons. They are named for their beautiful markings, which resemble an oriental carpet pattern.
